Her Contemporaries are Trash!

Her contemporaries are just not up to par with the abilities in which Beyonce beholds. Most put on sloppy and lazy performances, with a bunch of back-up dancers or flashing lights to distract from the fact of the lack of effort. They either rely on their vocals are their dancing, there is no in between. After a while seeing someone jump around the stage for hours tends to start to get boring. The obvious difference you see from after going to her concert and then going to another popstars is astonishing. Visual Effects, Marching Band, Rock Band, Back-Up Dancers, Back-Up Singers, Lights, Fire, Water, Confetti, Beyonce brings it all! Go big or Go home, and her contemporaries might as well just go home.
