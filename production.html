Beyonce has the Best Production

Beyonce invests heavily into her concerts. Beyonce has the best team in the game: Malcolm Weldon (Production Manager) Jake Berry (Production Manager) Es Devlin (Set designer) Tim Routledge (Lighting Designer) In Beyonce’s Formation World Tour there were an enormous custom designed stage including a treadmill runway, a B-stage pool filled with water, winches carrying acrobatic performers, a stage lift, and the full structure that supported the 70 foot video screens. For Coachella 2018 Beyonce had a pyramid stage designed for the show, a bleacher layered with dancers and musicians. A Beyonce concert is definitely worth seeing, next tour don’t miss out.
